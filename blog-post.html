How to find amazing Indian restaurants in your city
by siya shetty in Restaurants

To find amazing Indian restaurants in your city, start by exploring online review platforms like Yelp and TripAdvisor for highly-rated options and customer reviews. Social media, particularly Instagram, can also help you discover local favorites through food bloggers and hashtags. Don’t hesitate to ask friends or coworkers for their recommendations, as locals often know the best spots. Additionally, checking food delivery apps can provide insights into popular choices and menus. Finally, consider visiting local Indian markets to get recommendations from staff about nearby restaurants.

5 Tips on how to cook the best Roti
by Sejal Jagtap in Housecooking

1.Use Fresh Flour: Always use fresh whole wheat flour (atta) for the best texture and flavor. Sift the flour before use to aerate it.
2. Knead Properly: Knead the dough well until it’s smooth and elastic. This helps in developing gluten, making the roti soft. Let it rest for at least 20-30 minutes covered with a damp cloth.
3. Roll Evenly: Roll the dough into uniform circles of about 6-8 inches in diameter. Use a light touch to prevent the roti from becoming tough. Dust with flour to avoid sticking.
4. Cook on High Heat: Preheat your skillet or tawa until it’s hot. Cook the roti on high heat for 30 seconds on each side, then use a spatula to puff it up directly over the flame for a perfect texture.
5. Keep It Soft: After cooking, place the roti in a container lined with a clean cloth. This traps steam and keeps the roti soft until serving. You can also brush it with a little ghee or butter for added flavor.
